Our 3 Basic Scents

Cinnamon

We offer a very strong cinnamon smalling candle. This will fill your chosen room with that desired holiday feeling. You will feel Christmas yelling at you throughout every inch of your home. Only choose this if you want your entire house to be cinnamon.

Cherry

This scent is our best seller with candles in over 107,000 houses around the United States. It is like walking into a game of candy land after a long day at work. You will instantly be refreshed and ready to conquer the rest of the day.

Ocean Breeze

The name says it all, when you wake up you can expect to believe you are sitting on a beach somewhere with a drink beside you. Our ocean breeze scent is proven to enhance REM sleep and bring the customer to the most desirable dream world possible.
